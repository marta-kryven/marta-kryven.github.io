Welcome to our study!

IMPORTANT! This study runs on a desktop/laptop.

The study will NOT run on a mobile device.

You need to be focused to do this experiment, please make the browser window as big as you can and avoid distraction.

Multiple Object Tracking

You will see several moving objects presented in the center of the screen and a background image surrounding the objects.

Some of the objects are targets.

The target objects will blink before the animation starts. Others are distractors.

Your task is to track the targets and ignore the distractors.

When the animation stops, please click on the objects that you were tracking.

The animations starts slow, but gets faster later on. Please try to keep up!

The study is expected to take 10 minutes. Thanks for participating!
