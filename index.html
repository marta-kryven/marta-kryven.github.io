I am a computational cognitive scientist working with AI and experimental psychology. I design computational models of how people plan, think and perceive. In my most recent research I am using Large Language Models to understand cognition, and using cognition to understand Large Language Models. Check out my publications to see more about my projects.

I am always open to new collaborators - email me for potential collaboration or consulting inquiries.

Research interests

computational models of behaviour, spatial navigation, representation learning, program synthesis, computational creativity
